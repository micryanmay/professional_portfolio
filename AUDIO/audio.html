AUDIO

What You're Listening To

My interest in audio began when I attended the Audio Engineering program at the Grayson Technical Institute. From there it blossomed into a near obsession. My forte is audio editing and sound design, though I also have experience with foley artistry, field recording, studio recording (VO sessions, ADR sessions), MIDI sequencing, and music editing. I've completed work on several podcasts and webseries, and below you'll also find some 'for fun' examples of my work ranging from sound for picture to binaural sound design. For more information concerning each video please click the link to YouTube and read the video description there. All sound that you hear in all examples was created from scratch!
